Custom Spreadsheet Systems

Gotham Solutions builds practical spreadsheet systems designed around how your business actually works. Every project is custom built to organize information, automate repetitive work, and simplify daily processes.

Services

Business Tracking Systems

Track projects, clients, invoices, leads, tasks, or inventory using a system designed specifically for your workflow. Instead of juggling multiple documents, everything lives in one organized spreadsheet.

Spreadsheet Dashboards

Dashboards turn raw data into something you can actually understand. Custom dashboards help you track performance, monitor progress, and make decisions quickly without digging through spreadsheets.

Automation

Many business tasks involve the same calculations or steps repeated over and over. Automation removes manual work by using formulas, scripts, and structured systems that update automatically.

Workflow Organization

If your current spreadsheets feel scattered or difficult to manage, a custom workflow system can organize everything into one clear process that is easier to maintain and expand.

How Projects Work

1. Understanding Your Workflow

You describe what you currently track and what problems you want to solve. This helps identify what the system should do and how it should behave.

2. System Design

A spreadsheet system is designed around your workflow so it fits the way you already work rather than forcing you to adapt to a generic template.

3. Delivery and Adjustments

After the system is built, adjustments can be made to refine the workflow and ensure everything works smoothly.

What Makes Custom Spreadsheets Different

  • Built For Your Workflow
    Instead of adapting your process to a generic tool, the spreadsheet is designed around how your work actually happens.
  • Flexible
    Because everything runs in Google Sheets or Excel, your system can grow and change as your business evolves.
  • Simple and Maintainable
    The goal is always clarity. Systems are built to remain easy to understand and maintain long term.

Need a Custom System?

If you are currently managing information across multiple spreadsheets or doing repetitive manual work, a custom system may simplify your process considerably.
