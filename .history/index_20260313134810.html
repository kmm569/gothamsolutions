Custom Spreadsheet Design for Real Workflows

Many businesses rely on spreadsheets every day, but those files often grow over time without a clear structure. Information becomes scattered across multiple tabs, formulas become difficult to manage, and simple tasks start taking longer than they should.

Gotham Solutions focuses on designing custom spreadsheet systems that organize data clearly and reduce repetitive work. Instead of forcing your process into a generic template, each system is built around the way your business actually tracks information.

These systems are commonly used for client tracking, project management, invoice tracking, dashboards, and workflow organization. The goal is always the same: keep your data structured, accessible, and easy to maintain.
