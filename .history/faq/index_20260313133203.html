Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about custom spreadsheet systems and how Gotham Solutions works.

General Questions

What is a custom spreadsheet system?

A custom spreadsheet system is a spreadsheet built specifically around your workflow. Instead of using a generic template, the system is designed to track exactly the information your business needs while keeping everything organized.

Do you use Excel or Google Sheets?

Most systems are built using Google Sheets because it allows easy sharing and collaboration. However, Excel based solutions can also be created depending on your needs.

Can spreadsheets automate tasks?

Yes. Formulas and scripts can automate calculations, organize data automatically, generate summaries, and reduce manual work that would otherwise take time each day.

What kinds of systems can be built?

Common systems include client trackers, project management tools, invoice trackers, dashboards, lead trackers, and workflow management tools.

Working Together

How long does a project take?

Simple systems can often be completed quickly, while more complex workflows may take additional time depending on the features required.

Do I need technical knowledge?

No. Systems are designed to remain easy to use and understandable without requiring technical experience.

Can systems be updated later?

Yes. Because the systems are built using flexible spreadsheet tools, new features and improvements can be added over time as your needs evolve.

Still Have Questions?

If you are unsure whether a custom spreadsheet system would help your business, feel free to reach out and describe what you are currently trying to manage.
