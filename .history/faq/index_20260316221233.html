Frequently Asked Questions

Questions people commonly ask before starting a custom spreadsheet project. If you are unsure whether a custom system would help your workflow, the answers below should give you a clearer idea of what Gotham Solutions builds and how the process works.

About Custom Spreadsheets

How do I know if I actually need a custom spreadsheet?

If you already track information in spreadsheets but the system has become messy, confusing, or slow to update, a custom design can often simplify things significantly. Many clients come with a spreadsheet that partially works but has grown complicated over time. A redesign focuses on clarity and organization.

Can you improve an existing spreadsheet instead of building a new one?

Yes. Many projects begin with an existing spreadsheet that has evolved over time. Improving structure, formulas, and layout can make the system easier to maintain while keeping the information you already track.

What kinds of systems can be built?

Custom spreadsheet systems are commonly used for client tracking, project management, invoice tracking, lead management, workflow organization, and data dashboards. The structure depends entirely on what your business needs to track and how that information is used.

What is the difference between a template and a custom system?

Templates are designed for general use and often require adapting your workflow to match them. A custom spreadsheet is built around the way you already work, which usually makes it easier to manage and expand later.

Working With Gotham Solutions

How long does a project usually take?

The timeline depends on the complexity of the system. Simple tracking tools can often be completed quickly, while dashboards or larger workflow systems may require more time to design and test.

Will I still be able to edit the spreadsheet myself?

Yes. Custom spreadsheets are designed to remain understandable and maintainable. The goal is to give you a system that works better without making it difficult to update in the future.

Do I need technical experience to use a custom spreadsheet?

No. Systems are built with usability in mind. Most clients use the spreadsheet exactly like they would any normal document, but with a clearer structure and fewer manual steps.

Can the system grow as my workflow changes?

Yes. One advantage of spreadsheet based systems is flexibility. New columns, features, or tracking can be added later if your business evolves or your workflow changes.

Still Not Sure?

If you are currently managing information across multiple spreadsheets or repeating the same tasks every week, a custom system may help simplify your process. You can describe your current workflow and get feedback on whether a custom spreadsheet would make sense.
