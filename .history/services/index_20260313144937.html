What Gotham Solutions Builds

Most businesses already rely on spreadsheets, but they are often fragmented, difficult to maintain, or filled with manual work. Gotham Solutions focuses on turning those scattered spreadsheets into organized systems that actually support how a business runs.

Common Systems

Client and Lead Tracking

A centralized spreadsheet that tracks client information, contact history, project status, and notes. These systems replace scattered documents and help keep communication and progress organized.

Project and Task Management

For businesses managing multiple projects at once, custom spreadsheets can track deadlines, responsibilities, and progress in one place without requiring a complicated project management platform.

Financial and Invoice Tracking

Track invoices, payments, and revenue with systems designed to provide clear summaries and reporting. These spreadsheets can automatically calculate totals and highlight outstanding payments.

Data Dashboards

Dashboards consolidate important information into a single view. Instead of searching through multiple tabs, business owners can quickly see metrics that matter to them.

When a Custom Spreadsheet Helps

Your data is scattered

If information is spread across multiple spreadsheets, documents, or notes, consolidating it into a structured system can save significant time.

Your current spreadsheet is confusing

Many spreadsheets grow organically over time and become difficult to maintain. A redesign can simplify the layout and make the system easier to understand.

You repeat the same tasks constantly

Manual calculations, sorting, or formatting can often be automated so the spreadsheet updates itself.

How Custom Systems Are Different From Templates

  • Designed around your workflow
    Templates are built for general use. Custom systems reflect the exact information your business tracks.
  • Simpler to maintain
    A structured design reduces complexity and makes the system easier to update later.
  • Expandable
    Additional features or tracking can be added as your workflow evolves.

Have a Spreadsheet That Needs Improvement?

If you already use spreadsheets but feel they have become messy or inefficient, a custom redesign can often simplify the entire process.
