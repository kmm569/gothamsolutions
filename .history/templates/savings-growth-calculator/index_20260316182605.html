What this calculator does

This template helps you project future savings growth based on the numbers you already know. Instead of manually calculating compound interest, you can enter your starting balance, contributions, interest rate, and compounding schedule to see how your savings may grow over time.

It is designed to make planning easier whether you are building an emergency fund, comparing savings accounts, or working toward a long-term financial goal.

Best for

  • Personal savings planning
  • Emergency fund tracking
  • High-yield savings account projections
  • CD and fixed-rate growth estimates
  • Goal-based saving timelines
  • Comparing contribution strategies

Ways you can use it

  • Estimate savings growth over time
  • Compare saving strategies
  • See how long it takes to reach a financial goal
  • Understand the impact of interest compounding
  • Test different contribution amounts

How it works

  1. Open the spreadsheet template
  2. Enter your starting balance
  3. Add your monthly contribution amount
  4. Set the interest rate and compounding frequency
  5. Review the projected growth and timeline
