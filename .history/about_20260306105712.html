About Gotham Solutions

Gotham Solutions builds simple systems that help businesses organize their work and manage information more efficiently.

Many businesses rely on scattered notes, documents, or manual processes to track important information. Over time this can become difficult to manage and time-consuming.

Gotham Solutions creates structured tools that organize this information, simplify workflows, and make it easier to keep everything running smoothly.

These systems are built using Google Sheets, allowing them to stay flexible, accessible, and easy to adapt as your needs grow. Gotham Solutions can also improve existing spreadsheets, making them faster, cleaner, and easier to use.

Common projects include dashboards, trackers, report automation, and data cleanup designed around the way each business works.

Why Gotham Solutions

Every system is designed to be simple, practical, and built around how your business actually works.

  • Custom Built

  • Simple to Use
