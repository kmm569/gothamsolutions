About Gotham Solutions

Gotham Solutions focuses on building practical tools using Google Sheets and automation. Many businesses rely on spreadsheets every day but struggle with organization, workflow, or reporting.

Instead of replacing spreadsheets with expensive software, Gotham Solutions builds systems that make your existing tools faster, cleaner, and easier to use.

Common projects include dashboards, trackers, report automation, and data cleanup.
